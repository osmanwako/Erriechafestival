CELEBRATING IRREECHAA FESTIVAL 2023
Irreechaa is usually celebrated during the weekend following the Meskel holiday. The festival is observed in many areas of Oromia, but, at the national level, it brings millions of Oromos from all over Oromia and non-Oromo visitors from other parts of Ethiopia to the shores of Hora (Lake) Arsadi and, as of 2019, to Hora Finfinee in the capital.

This year we hope to expand our invitation to individuals and organizations including world class Medias to promote this beautiful culture to the rest of the world. By doing this we made the celebration festival to be the source of tourist attraction. So if you are interested to be part of celebration we hope you will consider join us this year to make Irreecha festival a world class celebrated festival.
